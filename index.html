Privacy Policy

How Aura collects, uses, and protects your information

Welcome to Aura. We built this app for students to connect, vibe, and express themselves within their school community. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it.

By using Aura, you agree to the practices described below. If you have any questions, reach out to us at the contact details at the bottom of this page.

1 Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Phone number (used for OTP verification and account identification)
  • First and last name
  • Username
  • Gender
  • Grade, school, and city
  • Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)

Information generated by your activity:

  • Vibe votes you cast (anonymous — your identity is never revealed to the recipient)
  • Friend connections you make
  • Aura points and leaderboard rankings
  • Sparks and keys earned through activity

Information collected automatically:

  • Device push notification token (for delivering notifications)
  • Basic usage data (e.g. app crashes, session events) for improving the service

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match you with students in your school for Vibe sessions
  • To show your profile to other students at your school
  • To deliver push notifications about your Aura activity
  • To maintain leaderboards, friend lists, and school communities
  • To improve the app and fix bugs
  • To prevent abuse and enforce our community guidelines

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3 Anonymity in Vibes

When another user receives a vibe vote, they are never told who voted for them. We only reveal limited demographic context (e.g. "a girl in grade 10") without identifying you. Your identity as the voter is protected at all times.

4 Data Sharing

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate Aura:

Firebase (Google) MSG91

  • Firebase — used for push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging) and profile photo storage (Firebase Storage). Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • MSG91 — used to send OTP verification SMS. Governed by MSG91's Privacy Policy.

These services only receive the data necessary to perform their function. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

5 Profile Photos

If you upload a profile photo, it is stored securely in Firebase Storage and is visible to other students within your school on Aura. You can update or remove your profile photo at any time from the Profile screen within the app.

6 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email below and we will process your request within 30 days.

7 Children and Minors

Aura is designed for school, college, and university students. If you are under 13, please do not use this app without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such data being collected, we will delete it promptly.

8 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by discontinuing use of the app

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

9 Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encrypted transit (HTTPS), secure storage, and access controls. No system is 100% secure, but we are committed to keeping your data safe.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date below. Continued use of Aura after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Child Safety Standards

Aura's standards against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE)

1 Our Commitment

Aura is built for school, college, and university students. The safety of minors is our highest priority. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

2 Prohibited Content and Behaviour

The following are strictly prohibited on Aura:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Sexual solicitation of minors
  • Grooming, exploitation, or predatory behaviour toward minors
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing explicit content involving anyone under 18
  • Any content that sexualises minors

Violations result in immediate account termination and reporting to the relevant authorities.

3 Reporting

Users can report child safety concerns by emailing us directly. All reports are reviewed promptly. Where required by law, we report confirmed CSAM to the National Centre for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant regional or national authorities.

To report a concern: com.infinite.aura@gmail.com

4 Prevention Measures

  • Aura is restricted to school-verified users — reducing exposure to unknown adults
  • User interactions are limited to people within the same school community
  • Profile photos and usernames are reviewed for policy compliance
  • Accounts of users found to be adults posing as minors are permanently banned

5 Compliance

Aura complies with all applicable child safety laws including the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act and other relevant regional legislation. We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations involving child safety.
